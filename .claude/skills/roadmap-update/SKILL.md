# Roadmap Update

## Goal

Keep `docs/roadmap.md` short, current, and useful for future planning.

## Procedure

1. Determine whether the item is active priority, backlog, completed, or deferred.
2. Keep active priority and backlog concise.
3. Put production-only/backend-owned work in the deferred section.
4. Keep mock-to-BFF/fetch integration final and deferred until a BFF exists.
5. Do not turn the roadmap into a session log.
6. Report changed priority/status and any assumptions.

## Guardrails

- Do not invent missing product decisions.
- Do not start roadmap items unless explicitly asked.
- Preserve backend ownership of financial, operational, and stock decisions.
